Endgame Gear OP1 8K

Click Latency

The Endgame Gear OP1 8k has exceptionally low and consistent click latency, providing an extremely responsive in-game experience for gaming in any genre. In the image it shows a test wherein the click latency is at 0.077 micro seconds.

8k Hz Polling Rate

Even though 8000 Hz polling can even be found on wireless mice these days, there are still good reasons to opt for a wired 8000 Hz mouse instead. For one, battery life is not a concern, and the connection will invariably be more stable as well.

Weight

This mouse is remarkably lightweight,with a weight of 49 grams and the weight is well-centered, making it easy to move quickly and accurately.
